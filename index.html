About me

Alexandra (Ali for short) is someone who has always been pretty curious. Often it works in her favor, but sometimes it can lead to trouble. She helped start a network of K-12 independent schools after spending a few years at a media agency trying to dig into the hearts and minds of people's preferences and behaviors. At work, her specialty is developing plans and campaigns and steps and systems to help people - and her schools - reach their goals.

Check out some of the projects that she is working on. Some of these things she does professionally and some of these things are just for fun...for now.
